ABOUT

Sketchy Animal Kingdom is an NFT project that brings together a community of love and support for the Animal Kingdom. Our goal is to raise awareness for sustaining the Animal Kingdom by connecting NFT holders to information about the animals, their food and their habitat in a fun way. We have made the project more accessible and sustainable to more people launching on the Polygon Network with very low gas fees and with a much smaller carbon footprint. Sketchy Badger is based on a Honey Badger and is the first animal NFT to launch. One day there will be a whole ecosystem of species including carnivores, omnivores and herbivores. Holding Sketchy Animals opens up access to more collectables in the future including prey and food tokens as the Kingdom grows. Are you ready to have fun rebuilding the Animal Kingdom while learning something new along the way?

THE ART

  • The animals are the superstars of this NFT collection. The NFT art is unique from many other collections.
  • Each image is a collage made from using the real animal as a base and hand drawn sketches combined in layers to make each piece of art an original creation.
  • The art is an auto generative collection of sketchy features and a fun way to interact with the animal.
  • The collages are inspired by Andy Warhol’s Endangered Species Series a collection of rare Pop Art from the early 80’s.
